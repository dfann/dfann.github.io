About Pondu.

Here at Pondu our goal is to improve your social experience as a college student, whether you are incoming freshman or enrolled in your school's graduate program.

We understand that a colege campus can be a strange and bizarre place, regardless of its size. However this is the time in your life to cultivate freindships, build life-long networks, and explore new opportunities. That is why at Pondu we hope to provide you with chances to discover yourself and the unique community around you.

What's the difference between Pondu and other apps?

Pondu is exclusive to college students. Designed to help them break social barries, meet new people and increase the encounters they have with other students on campus.

We understand the difficulties of a college student. Juggling school, your social life and activities such as work and sports. That is why our team works so hard to make things easier for you, allowing you to plan ahead and stay involved. We don't want you missing out on the most important part of college, the experience.

This isn't social media, it's Social Networking.

One of our most stressed and crucial objectives at Pondu, is to help college students physically go out and meet new people. Our app isn't designed to kepp you in your dorm room. We want you to explore campus.
